Ram Sukrut Kalyan Foundation is a grassroots NGO dedicated to improving education, healthcare, livelihoods, and social welfare across rural Maharashtra. Through community-led initiatives, health camps, skill-building programs, and educational support, we work towards building stronger and self-reliant villages.
